Tarot

One Card Reading

Tarot has been used for divination since the 18th century. Today, many people use tarot to explore the human psyche, particularly the unconscious mind. This tarot application can be used in a variety of ways and for a variety of reasons, but is for entertainment purposes only.

Think of a question or issue you would like to explore with this tarot reading. Click the button below to draw one tarot card from the Rider-Waite-Smith tarot deck. One card, its title, and its corresponding meaning will be provided, which can then be meditated upon now and throughout your day.
